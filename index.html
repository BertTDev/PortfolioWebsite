Refinements of the Flocking Algorithm via Spatial Acceleration Structures

Explored performance improvements to Boid simulations via the use of various spatial acceleration structures. From the ground up in C++ bin lattice subdivision and variations on Octree solutions were developed and profiled to complete a first class masters dissertation detailing implementation and enhancements to framerate and memory usage.

Table Tilter 2077 - Physics Engine + AI

Implemented a Physics Engine including Collision Detection and Resolution (via normal and frictional impulses), Rigid Body Physics and Constraints. Made use of this to develop a physics-based game, introducing state-based AIs relying on implementation of the A* pathfinding algorithm. Also coded collision body, Quadtree and A* visualisations to enable easier debugging.
